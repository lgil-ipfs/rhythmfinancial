About Rythm Financial

A practice built around your priorities, not ours.

Rythm Financial is an independent advisory practice serving clients across Vancouver and British Columbia. We provide objective, comprehensive financial guidance with no obligation to sell proprietary products — just the right solutions for your situation.

  • Truly Independent Access to the full Canadian insurance and investment market, not a captive shelf.
  • Long-Term Relationships We review and adapt your plan as your life evolves, not just at point of sale.
  • Compliant and Regulated CIRO registered mutual funds advisor. Licensed for insurance across BC and Ontario.
  • Education First We explain every recommendation clearly so you can make confident decisions.

How We Work

A structured path to
clarity and confidence.

We follow a consistent, transparent process designed to understand your full picture before recommending anything.

01
Discovery Meeting

A relaxed, no-commitment conversation to understand your goals, current coverage, family situation, and what keeps you up at night financially.

02
Needs Analysis

We conduct a thorough review of your existing policies, investment accounts, and protection gaps before any product is ever discussed.

03
Recommendations

We present a written plan with options, clear explanations, and full disclosure of compensation — so you can make an informed decision.

04
Implementation

We handle the applications, underwriting, and paperwork while keeping you informed at every step — no surprises.

05
Ongoing Review

Annual check-ins and proactive outreach when your life changes — because a plan built five years ago may not fit your life today.

The best financial plan is the one that actually fits how you live.
